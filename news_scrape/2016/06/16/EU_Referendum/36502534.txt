  Lots of you have got in touch with the BBC about the relationship between Britain's membership of the EU and current levels of migration.  According to the latest figures published by the Office for National Statistics (ONS), overall net migration stands at 333,000, and has risen more than a third since David Cameron came to power in 2010, with the aim of reducing numbers to tens of thousands a year.
