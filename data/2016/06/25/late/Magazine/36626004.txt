 A couple of weeks ago, the BBC visited the town of Romford, one of the most Eurosceptic towns in the UK.  It's in the London borough of Havering, where on Thursday 70% of voters chose to leave the European Union.  We've been back to the town to catch up with single mum Fay, who fiercely wanted to remain, and David the fishmonger, just one of the people celebrating their victory.
