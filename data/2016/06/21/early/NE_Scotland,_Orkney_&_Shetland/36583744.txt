 A man has died after the car he was travelling in crashed into a house in Aberdeenshire. The accident happened on the A947 Banff to Turriff road at Plaidy at about 20:20 on Saturday. Front seat passenger Leslie Stuart, 58, suffered serious injuries and was airlifted to Aberdeen Royal Infirmary but died on Monday. The 41-year-old man who was driving the car is in hospital with a fractured leg. Sgt Colin Matheson, of Police Scotland, said: "Our thoughts at this time are with the family and friends of those involved." Police have appealed to anyone who saw the collision or who saw a red Audi A4 estate car being driven on the A947 on Saturday evening to contact them.
