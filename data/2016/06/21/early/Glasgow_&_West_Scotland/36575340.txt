 A woman is being treated in hospital after being struck by a lorry on a busy Glasgow street. Police said the woman had been injured in the incident on Maryhill Road, near the Tesco store, at about 13:20. Paramedics attended and she was taken to Glasgow Royal Infirmary. There is no information available on her condition. Police closed off a section of Maryhill Road to investigate the crash but it has since re-opened.
