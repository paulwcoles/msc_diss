 There are long queues on the M4 heading towards Cardiff ahead of Beyonce's concert in the capital. There is stop-start traffic for eight miles on the westbound carriageway between junction 23 for the M48 and junction 26 for Malpas Road. Heavy traffic is also being reported eastbound on the Briton Ferry Bridge and the A40 in Monmouthshire. Beyonce's Formation world tour is at the Principality Stadium from 19:00 BST on Thursday.
