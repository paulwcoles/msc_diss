 Rescuers have found all 26 children who went missing in the Brecon Beacons. The children, in their mid teens and from St Albans, Hertfordshire, got lost around Llyn y Fan Fach, near Abercraf. Andrew Evans of Western Beacons Mountain Rescue Team said a rescue helicopter located the missing groups and every child has either made their way off the mountain or are on their way down. They have been taken to hospital for a precautionary check-up.
