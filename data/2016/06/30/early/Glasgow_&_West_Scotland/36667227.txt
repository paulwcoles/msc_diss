 A teenager has appeared in court charged with murder after a 16-year-old was stabbed near a school in Glasgow. Luke Wallace, 16, died a week after being attacked close to Bannerman High School in Baillieston on 17 June. The 16-year-old accused appeared in private at Glasgow Sheriff Court, where he made no plea or declaration. The teenager, who cannot be named for legal reasons, was granted bail and is expected to appear at the city's high court at a later date.
