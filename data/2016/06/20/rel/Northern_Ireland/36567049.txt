 A 24-year-old man has been charged in relation to an attack in north Belfast.  He will face a number of charges including aggravated burglary with intent to commit grievous bodily harm and common assault.  A man was injured and a house on Etna Drive had its front window smashed during the incident on Saturday morning.  A car outside the property was also damaged.  The 24-year-old is due to appear at Belfast Magistrates Court  on Monday morning.
