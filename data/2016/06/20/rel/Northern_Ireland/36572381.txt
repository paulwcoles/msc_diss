 A 25-year-old man has been charged over an attack on a man in Bangor on Sunday morning. A 36-year-old was assaulted in the High Street area at about 01:30 BST.  Police said he was taken to hospital for treatment on injuries that are not believed to be life threatening.  The accused will appear in Newtownards Magistrates' Court on Friday, 15 July, charged with grievous bodily harm with intent.
