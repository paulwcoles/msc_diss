 Wales supporters sing 'please don't send me home, I just don't want to go home' in the Paris fan zone. It follows their side's 1-0 victory over Northern Ireland at Euro 2016 and progression to the quarter finals. Up to 50,000 Wales fans travelled to Paris for the game, with many heading to the fan zone. Situated near the base of the Eiffel Tower, it is able to hold 90,000 people - almost double the capacity of the Parc des Princes stadium.
