 Wales football fans are celebrating witnessing history after the team reached the quarter finals of Euro 2016. Wales beat Northern Ireland 1-0 in the last 16 game at the Parc des Princes on Saturday thanks to an own goal. Wales went 1-0 up in the 75th minute after Gareth McAuley hit a ball fired across the goal by Gareth Bale. It is the first time Wales has made it to the knockouts of a major tournament since the 1958 World Cup. As the final whistle blew, fans in the Paris fan zone erupted.
