 Police are searching for a 33-year-old Castle Huntly inmate who has failed to return to the open prison following a period of home leave.  Officers said the public should not approach Thomas Proctor, who was reported missing at 15:00 on Wednesday from the jail near Dundee. Police Scotland said it was understood that he may have travelled to the Airdrie area. He was described as 6ft tall with short dark hair.
