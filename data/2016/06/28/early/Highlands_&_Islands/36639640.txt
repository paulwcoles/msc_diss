 A man has been taken to hospital after being spotted in the sea next to a dinghy near the shoreline at Fortrose. RNLI Kessock was alerted at 08:46 and went to the aid of the man, who was wearing a lifejacket. The man was taken ashore where he was given emergency first aid and further medical care when an ambulance arrived. He was airlifted to Raigmore Hospital in Inverness by a coastguard helicopter.
