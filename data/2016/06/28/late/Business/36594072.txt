 Uncertain business environments can be tough for companies to operate in.  Thailand is a good example of a volatile environment with companies based in the country having to cope with a series of natural disasters and coups. Yet entrepreneurship is thriving there. CEO coach Steve Tappin talks to Thai bosses including Paul Srivorakul, founder and chief executive of aCommerce, who started his business in the middle of a coup, to find out what global chief executives can learn.
