 Two people have been seriously injured after two vehicles crashed in Newport. Emergency services were called at about 07:35 BST to Malpas Road, near to its junction with Pillmawr Road. Gwent Police said one of the injured was taken to the Royal Gwent Hospital, Newport, and the other to the University Hospital of Wales, Cardiff. Malpas Road remains closed and officers are appealing for anyone who witnessed the collision to get in touch by ringing 101.
