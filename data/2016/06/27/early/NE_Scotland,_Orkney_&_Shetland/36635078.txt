 A 32-year-old man who died after his car crashed on Orkney has been named. Police said he was James Gunn, from Stromness. Mr Gunn's white Ford Focus crashed on the A967 at Garson Farm, Shandwick, on Saturday morning. Police have appealed for anyone who saw the car between Stromness and Twatt from 04:15 to 05:30 to come forward.
