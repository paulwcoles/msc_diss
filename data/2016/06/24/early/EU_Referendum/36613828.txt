 The first results of the EU referendum declared in the West Midlands has seen the region vote to leave the European Union. The first count to finish was in North Warwickshire where 67% of votes gave a clear win to the leave campaign. A total of 25,385 people voted to leave and 12,569 to remain after a 75% turnout. In Birmingham, the leave campaign won by a hair's breadth after 50.4% voted leave and 49.6% remain.
