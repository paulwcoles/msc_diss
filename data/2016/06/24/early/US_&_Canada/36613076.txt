 A man who lost his daughter to heroin became set on rescuing other young drug addicts.  At the weekend, John Cramsey and two friends were stopped by police driving a truck full of weapons into New York City. They told police they were intent on taking another girl out of the hands of a dealer. At his hometown in Pennsylvania, a complex picture emerges of a man who decided to fight back against the US heroin epidemic. Anna Bressanin reports
