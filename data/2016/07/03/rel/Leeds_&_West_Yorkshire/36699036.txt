 About 50 firefighters are dealing with a major blaze at a Morrisons supermarket depot in Yorkshire. The warehouse is on the Wakefield 41 Industrial Estate, close to Junction 41 of the M1, West Yorkshire Police said. Kenmore Road, Wakefield, is closed in both directions as fire crews tackle the flames. Around 80% of the  86,000 sq ft (8,000 Sq m) building is on fire, the fire service said. Debris is falling around half-a-mile from the site, according to some reports on Twitter.
