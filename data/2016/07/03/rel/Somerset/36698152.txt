 Aviation fans were able to see the Lynx helicopter fly for the last time after 40 years of Royal Navy service.  BBC reporter Imogen Sellers spoke to Commander Phil Richardson, from 815 Naval Air Squadron, about its history ahead of its decommissioning in March.  Enthusiasts were also given the chance to meet Ed Sutton, from the US Air Force Reserve, and Major Darius Stachurski, from the Polish Air Force, which was taking part for the first time.
