 Police are investigating the unexplained death of a man found in an alleyway in Gwynedd. Peter Jones, aged 60, was found face down in the alley which runs parallel with Bangor High Street in the early hours of Saturday. He was pronounced dead at hospital despite efforts to revive him. His family have been informed and are being supported by specially trained police officers. North Wales Police are appealing for witnesses or anyone who is aware of Mr Jones's whereabouts in the 24 hours before his death.
