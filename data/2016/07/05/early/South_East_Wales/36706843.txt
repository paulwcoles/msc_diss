 Traffic jams have eased after a road was blocked when a car overturned on a busy road in Cardiff. The A48 eastbound carriage is blocked near Gabalfa, which happened at about 16:30 BST between the A470 North Road (Gabalfa interchange) and A4161 Southern Way (Llanedeyrn interchange). The road was blocked for about two hours but one lane has reopened and delays have eased. It is not yet known if anyone was injured.
