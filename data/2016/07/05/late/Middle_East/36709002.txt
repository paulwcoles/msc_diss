 A suicide attack has struck the Saudi city of Medina, close to the Prophet's Mosque, one of Islam's holiest sites, according to local media reports. A bomber detonated his device as security officers were breaking their Ramadan fast, al-Arabiya TV said. Footage on the TV network showed a blazing vehicle.  The mosque is the burial place of the Prophet Muhammad and Medina the second-holiest city in Islam after Mecca.
