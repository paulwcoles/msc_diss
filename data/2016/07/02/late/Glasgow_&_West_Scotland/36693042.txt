 A man has been arrested in connection with a shooting in Hamilton in South Lanarkshire. Two men suffered serious head and face injuries in an incident on Monday on a footpath between Millgate Road and Westwood Crescent in the town. A 25-year-old man is expected to appear at Hamilton Sheriff Court on Monday. One of the injured men, aged 26, was being treated in hospital in East Kilbride. The other, a 27-year-old, was in hospital in Glasgow.
