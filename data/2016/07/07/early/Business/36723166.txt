 Africans living outside of the continent often send money home to help out relatives. Overseas remittances are a big business in Africa and elsewhere.  But an entrepreneur in Accra in Ghana has gone a step further.  Instead of simply transferring money to a loved one's bank account back home, his clients can get a bespoke service where, within reason and the law, almost anything can be done.
