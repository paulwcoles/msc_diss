 Actors Catherine Zeta-Jones and David Hasselhoff have wished the Wales team good luck ahead of their Euro 2016 semi-final match with Portugal. Zeta-Jones, who is from Swansea, said the players had already done the country proud, while Hasselhoff, whose fiancee Hayley Roberts is from Neath Port Talbot, said "good luck Cymru, go Wales". Wales play Portugal in Lyon at 20:00 BST for a place in the final on Sunday.
