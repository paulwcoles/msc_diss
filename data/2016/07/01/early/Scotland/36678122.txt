 Police in Shetland have arrested and charged a 28-year-old man in connection with the sudden death of another man. The body of a 34-year-old man, who has not been named, was discovered at a property in the Cunningsburgh area on Saturday night. The death was previously treated as unexplained. A report has now been submitted to the procurator fiscal and the arrested man is due to appear at Lerwick Sheriff Court on Friday.
